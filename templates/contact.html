Quick answers to common questions

Booking a tour with us is easy! Simply browse our available tours, select the one you're interested in, and click the "Book Now" button. You can also contact us directly by phone, email, or through our contact form if you have specific questions or requirements.

Our tour prices typically include accommodation, transportation during the tour, guided activities, entrance fees to attractions, and some meals as specified in the itinerary. International flights, travel insurance, visa fees, and personal expenses are usually not included. Each tour page has a detailed "What's Included" section for specific information.

Our standard cancellation policy provides a full refund if canceled 60 days or more before departure, a 50% refund if canceled 30-59 days before departure, and no refund if canceled less than 30 days before departure. We recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment. Please note that some tours may have different cancellation terms, which will be clearly specified.
