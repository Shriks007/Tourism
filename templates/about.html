Our Story

Wanderlust was born from a simple yet powerful idea: travel has the ability to transform lives. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate travelers who had explored over 70 countries collectively, we set out to create a travel company that offers more than just sightseeing tours.

Our founders, having experienced the transformative power of immersive travel, wanted to share authentic, meaningful experiences with others. They believed that travel should connect people to new cultures, traditions, and perspectives in ways that foster understanding and appreciation.

Over the years, we've grown from a small team operating a handful of tours to a global company offering experiences in destinations across six continents. Yet, our core values remain unchanged - we're committed to responsible tourism, authentic experiences, and creating journeys that inspire and transform.

Our Commitment to Responsible Tourism

At Wanderlust, we believe that travel should be a force for good. We're committed to operating in a way that respects and benefits the people, environment, and economies of the places we visit.

Supporting Local Communities

We partner with local businesses, employ local guides, and contribute to community projects in the destinations we visit.

Environmental Responsibility

We minimize waste, offset carbon emissions, and choose eco-friendly accommodations and transportation options whenever possible.

Cultural Preservation

We educate our travelers about local customs and encourage respectful cultural exchange that preserves traditions and heritage.
